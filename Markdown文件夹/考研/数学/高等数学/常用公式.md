# 常用公式

## 极限四则运算

$$
\begin{aligned}
&\lim _{n \rightarrow c}[f(n)+g(n)]=\lim _{n \rightarrow c} f(n)+\lim _{n \rightarrow c} g(n)
\\
&\lim _{n \rightarrow c}[f(n)-g(n)]=\lim _{n \rightarrow c} f(n)-\lim _{n \rightarrow c} g(n)
\\
&lim _{n \rightarrow c}[f(n) \cdot g(n)]=\lim _{n \rightarrow c} f(n) \cdot \lim _{n \rightarrow c} g(n) 
\\
&\lim _{n \rightarrow c} \frac{f(n)}{g(n)}=\frac{\lim _{n \rightarrow c} f(n)}{\lim _{n \rightarrow c} g(n)}
\end{aligned}
$$

等式成立当且仅当右式极限存在且不为无穷。

## 正切，余切，正割，余割

- 正割： $\sec = 1/\cos$ ，读作secant[ˈsiːkənt]
- 余割： $\csc = 1 / \sin$ ，读作cosecant[kəʊˈsiːk(ə)nt]
- 正切： $\tan = \sin / \cos$ 
- 余切： $\cot = \cos / \sin$ 读作cotangent

## 一般求导法则

### 线性法则

$$
\begin{array}{l}
\frac{\mathrm{d}(M f)}{\mathrm{d} x}=M \frac{\mathrm{d} f}{\mathrm{~d} x} ; \quad[M f(x)]^{\prime}=M f^{\prime}(x) \\
\frac{\mathrm{d}(f \pm g)}{\mathrm{d} x}=\frac{\mathrm{d} f}{\mathrm{~d} x} \pm \frac{\mathrm{d} g}{\mathrm{~d} x}
\end{array}
$$

### 乘法定则

$$
\frac{\mathrm{d} f g}{\mathrm{~d} x}=\frac{\mathrm{d} f}{\mathrm{~d} x} g+f \frac{\mathrm{d} g}{\mathrm{~d} x}
$$

### 除法定则

$$
\frac{\mathrm{d} \frac{f}{g}}{\mathrm{~d} x}=\frac{\frac{\mathrm{d} f}{\mathrm{~d} x} g-f \frac{\mathrm{d} g}{\mathrm{~d} x}}{g^{2}} \quad(g \neq 0)
$$

### 倒数定则

$$
\frac{\mathrm{d} \frac{1}{g}}{\mathrm{~d} x}=\frac{-\frac{\mathrm{d} g}{\mathrm{~d} x}}{g^{2}} \quad(g \neq 0)
$$

### 复合函数求导法则（连锁定则）

$$
\begin{array}{l}
(f \circ g)^{\prime}(x)=f^{\prime}(g(x)) g^{\prime}(x) \\
\frac{\mathrm{d} f[g(x)]}{\mathrm{d} x}=\frac{\mathrm{d} f(g)}{\mathrm{d} g} \frac{\mathrm{d} g}{\mathrm{~d} x}=f^{\prime}[g(x)] g^{\prime}(x)
\end{array}
$$

### 反函数的导数

$$
\begin{aligned}
&\text { 由于 } g(f(x))=x, \text { 故 } g(f(x))^{\prime}=1, \text { 根据复合函数求导法则, 则 } g(f(x))^{\prime}=\frac{\mathrm{d} g[f(x)]}{\mathrm{d} x}=\frac{\mathrm{d} g(f)}{\mathrm{d} f} \frac{\mathrm{d} f}{\mathrm{~d} x}=1\\
&\text { 所以 } \frac{\mathrm{d} f}{\mathrm{~d} x}=\frac{1}{\frac{\mathrm{d} g(f)}{\mathrm{d} f}}=\left[\frac{\mathrm{d} g(f)}{\mathrm{d} f}\right]^{-1}=\left[g^{\prime}(f)\right]^{-1}\\
&\text { 同理 } \frac{\mathrm{d} g}{\mathrm{~d} x}=\frac{1}{\frac{\mathrm{d} f(g)}{\mathrm{d} g}}=\left[\frac{\mathrm{d} f(g)}{\mathrm{d} g}\right]^{-1}=\left[f^{\prime}(g)\right]^{-1}
\end{aligned}
$$

### 广义幂法则

$$
\left(f^{g}\right)^{\prime}=\left(e^{g \ln f}\right)^{\prime}=f^{g}\left(g^{\prime} \ln f+\frac{g}{f} f^{\prime}\right)
$$

## 代数函数的导数

(n为任意实常数)
$$
\begin{array}{l}
\frac{\mathrm{d} M}{\mathrm{~d} x}=0 \\
\frac{\mathrm{d} x^{n}}{\mathrm{~d} x}=n x^{n-1} \quad x \neq 0 \\
\frac{\mathrm{d}|x|}{\mathrm{d} x}=\frac{x}{|x|}=\operatorname{sgn} x \quad x \neq 0
\end{array}
$$

## 指数和对数函数的导数

$$
\begin{aligned}
\frac{d}{d x} e^{f(x)}&=f^{\prime}(x) e^{f(x)} \\
\\\\
\frac{\mathrm{d} e^{x}}{\mathrm{~d} x} &=\lim _{\Delta x \rightarrow 0} \frac{e^{x}-e^{x-\Delta x}}{\Delta x} \\
&=e^{x} \lim _{\Delta x \rightarrow 0} \frac{1-e^{-\Delta x}}{\Delta x} \\
&=e^{x}
\\
\\\\
\frac{\mathrm{d} \alpha^{x}}{\mathrm{~d} x} &=\frac{\mathrm{d} e^{x \ln \alpha}}{\mathrm{d} x} \\
&=\frac{\mathrm{d} e^{x \ln \alpha}}{\mathrm{d} x \ln \alpha} \cdot \frac{\mathrm{d} x \ln \alpha}{\mathrm{d} x} \\
&=e^{x \ln \alpha} \ln \alpha \\
&=\alpha^{x} \ln \alpha \\
\\\\
\frac{\mathrm{d} \ln x}{\mathrm{~d} x} &=\lim _{h \rightarrow 0} \frac{\ln (x+h)-\ln x}{h} \\
&= \lim _{h \rightarrow 0}\left(\frac{1}{h} \ln \left(\frac{x+h}{x}\right)\right) \\
&= \lim _{h \rightarrow 0}\left(\frac{x}{x h} \ln \left(1+\frac{h}{x}\right)\right) \\
&= \frac{1}{x} \ln \left(\lim _{h \rightarrow 0}\left(1+\frac{h}{x}\right)^{\frac{x}{h}}\right) \\
&= \frac{1}{x} \ln e \\
&=\frac{1}{x} \\
\\\\
\frac{\mathrm{d} \log _{\alpha}|x|}{\mathrm{d} x}&=\frac{1}{\ln \alpha} \frac{\mathrm{d} \ln |x|}{\mathrm{d} x}=\frac{1}{x \ln \alpha} \\
\frac{\mathrm{d} x^{x}}{\mathrm{~d} x}&=x^{x}(1+\ln x)
\end{aligned}
$$



## 三角函数的导数

$$
\begin{aligned}
(\sin x)^{\prime} &=\lim _{h \rightarrow 0} \frac{\sin (x+h)-\sin x}{h} \\
&=\lim _{h \rightarrow 0} \frac{\sin x \cos h+\cos x \sin h-\sin x}{h} \\
&=\lim _{h \rightarrow 0}\left(\sin x \frac{\cos h-1}{h}+\cos x \frac{\sin h}{h}\right) \\
&=\cos x \\
\\\\
(\cos x)^{\prime} 
&=\sin \frac{\cos (x+h)-\cos x}{h} \\
&=\lim _{h \rightarrow 0} \frac{\cos x \cos h-\sin x \sin h-\cos x}{h} \\
&=\lim _{h \rightarrow 0}\left(\cos x \frac{\cos h-1}{h}-\sin x \frac{\sin h}{h}\right) \\
&=-\sin x \\
\\\\
(\tan x)^{\prime} &=\left(\frac{\sin x}{\cos x}\right)^{\prime} \\
&=\frac{(\sin x)^{\prime} \cos x-\sin x(\cos x)^{\prime}}{\cos ^{2} x} \\
&=\frac{\cos ^{2} x-\sin ^{2} x}{\cos ^{2} x} \\
&=\frac{1}{\cos ^{2} x}=\sec ^{2} x 
\\
\\\\
(\cot x)^{\prime} &=\left(\frac{\cos x}{\sin x}\right)^{\prime} \\
&=\frac{(\cos x)^{\prime} \sin x-\cos x(\sin x)^{\prime}}{\sin ^{2} x} \\
&=\frac{-\sin ^{2} x-\cos ^{2} x}{\sin ^{2} x} \\
&=-\frac{1}{\sin ^{2} x}=-\csc ^{2} x
\\\\
(\sec x)^{\prime} &=\left(\frac{1}{\cos x}\right)^{\prime} \\
&=\frac{\sin x}{\cos ^{2} x} \\
&=\sec x \tan x \\
\\\\
(\csc x)^{\prime} &=\left(\frac{1}{\sin x}\right)^{\prime} \\
&=\frac{-\cos x}{\sin ^{2} x} \\
&=-\csc x \cot x
\end{aligned}
$$

## 反三角函数导数

$$
\begin{aligned}
(\arcsin x)^{\prime} &=\frac{1}{\cos (\arcsin x)} \Leftrightarrow \sin (\arcsin x)=x \Leftrightarrow \cos (\arcsin x)(\arcsin x)^{\prime}=1 \\
&=\frac{1}{\sqrt{1-\sin ^{2}(\arcsin x)}} \\
&=\frac{1}{\sqrt{1-x^{2}}}(|x|<1) \\
\\\\
(\arccos x)^{\prime} &=\frac{1}{-\sin (\arccos x)} \Leftrightarrow \cos (\arccos x)=x \Leftrightarrow-\sin (\arccos x)(\arccos x)^{\prime}=1 \\
&=-\frac{1}{\sqrt{1-\cos ^{2}(\arccos x)}} \\
&=-\frac{1}{\sqrt{1-x^{2}}}(|x|<1) \\
\\\\
(\arctan x)^{\prime} &=\frac{1}{\sec ^{2}(\arctan x)} \Leftrightarrow \tan (\arctan x)=x \Leftrightarrow \sec ^{2}(\arctan x)(\arctan x)^{\prime}=1 \\
&=\frac{1}{1+\tan ^{2}(\arctan x)} \\
&=\frac{1}{1+x^{2}} \\
\\\\
(\operatorname{arccot} x)^{\prime}&=\frac{1}{-\csc ^{2}(\operatorname{arccot} x)} \Leftrightarrow \cot (\operatorname{arccot} x)=x \Leftrightarrow-\csc ^{2}(\operatorname{arccot} x)(\operatorname{arccot} x)^{\prime}=1\\
&=-\frac{1}{1+\cot ^{2}(\operatorname{arccot} x)} \\
&=-\frac{1}{1+x^{2}}\\
\\\\
(\operatorname{arcsec} x)^{\prime} &=\frac{1}{\sec (\operatorname{arcsec} x) \tan (\operatorname{arcsec} x)} \Leftrightarrow \sec (\operatorname{arcsec} x)=x \Leftrightarrow \sec (\operatorname{arcsec} x) \tan (\operatorname{arcsec} x)(\operatorname{arcsec} x)^{\prime}=1 \\
&=\frac{1}{|x| \sqrt{\sec ^{2}(\operatorname{arcsec} x)-1}} \\
&=\frac{1}{|x| \sqrt{x^{2}-1}}(|x|>1) \\
\\\\
(\operatorname{arcsc} x)^{\prime} &=\frac{1}{-\csc (\operatorname{arccsc} x) \cot (\operatorname{arccsc} x)} \Leftrightarrow \csc (\operatorname{arccsc} x)=x \Leftrightarrow-\csc (\operatorname{arccsc} x) \cot (\operatorname{arccsc} x)(\operatorname{arccsc} x)^{\prime}=1 \\
&=-\frac{1}{|x| \sqrt{\csc ^{2}(\operatorname{arcsec} x)-1}} \\
&=-\frac{1}{|x| \sqrt{x^{2}-1}}(|x|>1)
\end{aligned}
$$

## 高斯积分
$$
\begin{aligned}
&\int\limits_{-\infty} ^\infty  e^{-t^2}dt = \sqrt{\pi},\int\limits_{0} ^\infty  e^{-t^2}dt = \frac{\sqrt{\pi}}{2}
\\
&\int\limits_{-\infty} ^x  e^{-t^2}dt \ \text{无法积分}
\\
&\int\limits_{-\infty} ^x te^{-t^2}dt = \left. -\frac{1}{2}e^{-t^2}\right|^x_{-\infty}
\\
&\int\limits_{-\infty}^x t^2 e^{-t^2}dt \ \text{无法积分}
\\
& \int\limits_{-\infty}^\infty t^2e^{-t^2} dt = \int\limits_{-\infty}^\infty -(\frac{t}{2}) de^{-t^2} = \left.-\frac{t}{2}e^{-t^2}\right| +\frac{1}{2} \int\limits_{-\infty} ^\infty  e^{-t^2}dt = \frac{\sqrt{\pi}}{2}
\end{aligned}
$$

## 洛必塔法则

函数 $f,g$ 在以 $c$ 为端点的开区间可微，且 $\lim _{x \rightarrow c} \frac{f^{\prime}(x)}{g^{\prime}(x)} \in \overline{\mathbb{R}}$ ，并且 $ g^{\prime}(x) \neq 0$ 。

此时有两种形式可以使用洛必塔法则 $\lim\limits_{x\rightarrow c}\frac{f(x)}{g(x)} = \lim\limits_{x\rightarrow c}\frac{f'(x)}{g'(x)}$ ：

> $\lim\limits_{x\rightarrow c}f(x) = 0,\lim\limits_{x\rightarrow c}g(x) = 0$
>
> $\lim\limits_{x\rightarrow c}|f(x)| = \infty,\lim\limits_{x\rightarrow c}|g(x)| = \infty$
